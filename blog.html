I would drown like my peers
By Jackie Xu | 2 Comments

The key danger posed by climate change is malnutrition or starvation due to habitat loss. We swim from a platform of sea ice. Rising temperatures cause the sea ice to melt earlier in the year. Reduction in sea-ice cover also forces us to swim longer distances, which further depletes our energy stores and occasionally leads to drowning. My friend Jim has drowned last week. I feel so sad to lose my dear friend.
